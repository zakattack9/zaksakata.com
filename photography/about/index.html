About Me
Growing up in Hawaii, I always enjoyed capturing vibrant scenery popping with color. From sunsets to cities, it has always been an adventure exploring new types of photography and capturing fleeting moments.

Taking pictures on trips and editing photos when time allowed, I've always considered photography a side passion of mines. With inspiration from other fellow photographers, I love composing pictures with interesting subjects that burst with bright hues.

My gallery of photographs has become a visual representation of personal progress, improving with every adjustment and click of a shutter.
